About Me


Hi! I’m May Joy Agunod, a BS Information Technology student and the creator of Coding Corner. My goal is to make coding more approachable and fun for everyone — especially for students who might be struggling with their first lines of code.


What You’ll Find Here:


  • Easy tutorials on HTML, CSS, and Java
  • Tips on improving problem-solving skills
  • Motivation to keep learning despite challenges

“Don’t be afraid to fail — every error message is a step closer to success.”
